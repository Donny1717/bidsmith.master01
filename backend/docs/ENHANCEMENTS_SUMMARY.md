# BidSmith Enhancements Summary

## Overview

BidSmith has been comprehensively enhanced with detailed knowledge bases covering all UK construction and NHS tender requirements.

## ✅ Completed Enhancements

### 1. CDM 2015 - Enhanced with Regulation-Specific Duties
- All duty holders (Client, Principal Designer, Principal Contractor, Designers, Contractors) with specific regulation references
- Pre-construction information, Construction Phase Plan, Health and Safety File
- Mandatory statements for each duty holder
- Complete compliance validation

### 2. HSWA 1974 - Complete Section Coverage
- Section 2 - Employer duties to employees (with all subsections 2(2)(a) through 2(2)(e) and 2(6))
- Section 3 - Duties to non-employees
- Sections 7-8 - Employee duties
- Proposal integration elements:
  - Health & Safety Policy
  - Training Programs
  - Supervision Structure
  - Risk Management Approach

- Part A: Structure

- Part G: Sanitation
- Part H: Drainage
- Part J: Heat Producing Appliances
- Part K: Protection from Falling
- Part L: Conservation of Fuel and Power
- Part M: Access
- Part P: Electrical Safety
- Part Q: Security

#### Quality Documentation

#### Insurance Documentation

- Financial standing evidence

- Financial capacity

#### Certifications and Accreditations
- ISO certifications
- CHAS, Constructionline, Achilles, SafeContractor
- SSIP membership

#### References and Case Studies
- Client references (2-3 typically)
- Relevant project experience
- Case studies
